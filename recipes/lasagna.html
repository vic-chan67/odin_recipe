Lasagna

Description:

This recipe has 12 servings and takes a total time of 3 hours and 15 minutes to prep and cook.

Ingrediants:

  • 1 3/4 pound lean ground beef
  • 1/2 cup minced onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 13 ounces canned tomato sauce
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 4 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
  • 16 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 3/4 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 12 lasagna noodles

Steps:

  1. Cook ground beef, onions and garlic over medium heat until well browned
  2. Stir in tomato sauce and water. Season with sugar, parsly, basil, salt, and pepper. Simmer, covered, for about 1 hour, stirring occasionally
  3. Cook lasagna noodles in boiling water for approximately 10 minutes. Drain noodles, and rinse with colde water
  4. Combine ricotta cheese with parsly, and salt
  5. Spread some meat sauce on the bottom of a baking dish. Cover with a few sheets of lasagna noodles. Spread ricotta cheese mix on top of the lasagna noodles. Repeat until all mixtures are gone
  6. Bake lasagna in oven for 25 minutes at 190 degrees Celsius
