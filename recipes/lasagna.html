Lasagna

Description:

This recipe has 12 servings and takes a total time of 3 hours and 15 minutes to prep and cook.

Ingrediants:

  • 1 3/4 pound lean ground beef
  • 1/2 cup minced onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 13 ounces canned tomato sauce
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 4 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
  • 16 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 3/4 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 12 lasagna noodles
