Gluten-free flatbread

Description:

Makes 8 pieces of flatbread that only takes 20 minutes of prep and cooking.

Ingredients:

  • 400g gluten-free self-raising flour, plus extra for dusting
  • 1 tbsp cumin seeds, toasted
  • 300ml natural yogurt

Steps:

  1. Heat the grill to medium and dust a baking sheet with a little flour
  2. Mix the flour and cumin seeds in a bowl, then season
  3. Stir in the yogurt and 100ml water, then mix to form a soft dough
  4. Divide the dough into 8 equal pieces, then shape into circles or ovals
  5. Grill on the baking sheet for 3-5 mins of each side until golden and puffed
